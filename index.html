Welcome!

Thank you for your interest in my survey. It will take about 2-4 minutes to complete and explores how players perceive variation in the presentation of video games.

The survey consists of 2 questions, each containing a side-by-side comparison between 2 short video clips. You are tasked with watching both of them and selecting the one you prefer visually. If you cannot see any differences or if you have no clear preference for one of them, please choose "No Preference". You may replay the videos as often as you like, there is no time limit.

Participation is possible using any device, however, a bigger screen is recommended to help you observe the presented scenes in more detail.

Please only participate in the survey once.
This survey is anonymous. Apart from your responses, no data is collected.
